Football, known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net. The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. It’s known for its fast-paced action, skillful play, and global popularity, with major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup showcasing the sport’s highest level of competition.
